The rules

How this ledger works

✓

Loud and graded

Every call is made on air, in the episode, before the outcome is known — then graded against primary sources. No quiet edits, no retroactive hedging.

⚖

Boldness counts

A safe call graded right is worth less than a bold one. Grades weigh how contrarian the call was when Spike made it, not just whether it landed.

∞

Misses are permanent

A correction gets the same screen time as the original claim. Wrong calls stay on this board forever — that's what makes the right ones worth anything.
